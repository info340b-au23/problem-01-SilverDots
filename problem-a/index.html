Bocchi the Rock (Hitori Gotoh)

Who is Bocchi the Rock?

Hitori Gotoh, more affectionately recognized by fans as titular protagonist of Bocchi the Rock, is the lead guitarist of Kessoku band. Often seen sporting a pink jersey that matches her hair color, Bocchi, despite her introverted personality, enjoys the company of her friends and fellow bandmates Nijika, Ryo, and Ikuyo. Everyday's a new experience for the young Bocchi as she learns to conquer her fears and perform on the grand stage.
